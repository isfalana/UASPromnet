About

ES TUKUL (Tuku kul-kul)

Es kulkul is a traditional Indonesian iced snack that has been around for a long time. The name kulkul comes from the words shake or kukul, which refers to the way this ice is made. Kulkul ice was first introduced in traditional markets and became very popular among elementary school children. This snack is not only popular among elementary school children but adults also really enjoy it. Why did this happen???

Because of ES TUKUL (tuku kul-kul)which serves kul-kul ice with fresh fruit and sweet chocolate that will tempt the taste buds of adults and children. ES TUKUL is served with many toppings, namely:

  • messes
  • springkle
  • oreo
